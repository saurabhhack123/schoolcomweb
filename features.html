The SchoolCom SmartTab is an Android-tablet based data capture, analytics and messaging platform that makes measuring student performance and managing day-to-day school activities easy for teachers, administrators, parents and students

The robust messaging facility built into the platform also allows easy broadcast of messages and enables parents to keep a close track on their child’s performance, also adding an element of trust and safety.

SchoolCom SmartTab for Teachers

Easy Data Capture

Teachers can use inbuilt forms for marks and attendance capture, or create customized forms using the intuitive form builder.

Easy Dashboards & Navigation

Interactive dashboards and navigation allow teachers to view micro level data and perform analytics to perform comparison.

Exam & Homework Management

Teachers can use inbuilt forms for marks and attendance capture, or create customized forms using the intuitive form builder.

Co-Scholastic and CCE Proﬁles

Customizable forms make it easy to administer and capture data from tests and homework, making it available for advanced analytics.

SchoolCom SmartTab for the Principal

Attendance Management

Advanced analytics dashboards allow principals to observe attendance trends in real time and take remedial actions when required.

Performance Management

Dashboards allow Principals to view performance data and perform trend analysis and comparisons to judge curriculum and teaching eﬃciency.

Messaging

Robust messaging platform makes it easy for Principals to broadcast messages at various levels, removing the hassle and time consumed in communication.
